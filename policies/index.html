Policies

Trade-In Policies

We do not pay cash for books. We only offer store credit for trade-ins.

We accept any/all books in good condition, all genres and age ranges, with the exception of visually graphic or explicit material. We reserve the right to refuse books that appear to be moldy, mildewed, water-damaged, or otherwise unsellable. Refused books will not be eligible for credit.

For large loads of books we offer $5 in store credit per box. We are currently limited in our ability to take multiple boxes at a time and will make a decision on a case-by-case basis. Please fill out our Contact Us form or message us on social media for more information.

For individual books in like-new condition our trade-in credit policy is as follows:

  • Hardcovers with dust jacket: 37.5% of the cover price
  • Hardcovers without dust jacket: 30% of the cover price
  • Paperbacks: 25% of the cover price

    • Books in well-loved condition will receive $1 in store credit.

    Store credit is usable for up to 50% of the cost of any merchandise purchase.
