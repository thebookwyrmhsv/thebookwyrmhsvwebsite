FAQ

  1. What kind of books do y'all accept?
    After January 6th, we will accept books of any genre and type so long as they are in sellable condition.
  • When can I bring books by?
    Check out our Facebook page for the most accurate information on store hours and more.
  • I stopped by but y'all were closed. Can I leave my books on the sidewalk by your door?
    PLEASE do not leave books unattended outside the door. This creates too much potential for pests, water damage, and other issues that will render your books unsellable. Also, if we don't meet you when you drop the books off, we can't give you store credit!
  • My group is looking for a place to hold a weekly/monthly/repeating meeting. Do you offer discounts?
    YES! Please message us on Facebook or get in touch with us using the form on our Contact Us page for more details.
  • How does pricing work?
    Currently the discounts for books in like-new condition is 25% off the cover price for hardcovers with dust jacket, 40% off hardcovers without dust jacket, and 50% off all paperbacks.
    For bargain books that have been well loved, prices range beween $1-5.
  • What's the best way for out-of-towners to support the store?
    Check out our Bookshop.org and Libro.fm affiliate links below!
    Note for our overseas friends: Bookshop.org currently only ships in the US and the UK.
  • I have a question that's not on the list. How do I get in touch with you?
    Either message us on social media or use our Contact Us form.
